pH Measuring Kit

The pH level of something tells us how acidic or basic it is. The pH scale goes from 0 to 14.

Measuring the pH of a liquid or of the soil is important. It helps us understand everything that happens around it. It is important in water and soil quality, growing healthy plants, making food and drinks, and chemistry.

The pH Measuring kit is used to measure the pH of a substance. It tells you how acidic or basic a liquid, or the soil. You can learn about water, soil, and the world around you using a pH Measuring Kit.

05/XX

Safety Considerations

When using equipment, we need to always be safe

Read and follow the below safety instructions when using this kit

Storage and Cleaning

Wash the water pH meter with distilled water after each use so it stays accurate.

Safe Handling and Storage

The water pH meter has glass parts and can break. Notify an adult of any spills or broken glass.

Store chemicals in a safe place away from reach of children and pets.

Store water pH meter in a special solution to avoid it drying out and breaking.

Protect your eyes

Wear safety goggles when mixing chemicals with dirt.

Use special liquids called buffer solutions (pH 4, 7, or 10) to keep your water pH meter reading correctly.
